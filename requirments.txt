The following fields should be part of resume.

PersonalInformation
    1. Name
    2. Job Title
    3. Email
    4. Phone
    5. Location
    6. summary

Experience (Can have multiple Experiences)
    1. Employer
    2. Job Title
    3. City
    4. Start date
    5. End date
    6. Description

Skills (Can have multiple skills)
    1. Skill Name

Education (Can have multiple Education details)
    1. Degree
    2. College Name
    3. City
    4. cgpa
